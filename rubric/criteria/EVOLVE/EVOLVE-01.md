# EVOLVE-01 — The practice is documented

## Criterion

The team's practice for producing code with AI assistance — the tools, the workflow, the specification format, the review process — is documented in a durable artefact within the codebase or an addressable equivalent.

## Why it matters

An undocumented practice cannot be evolved deliberately; it can only be changed by drift. Documentation is the precondition for anyone outside the current team — including the current team six months from now — to understand what is being asked of them.

## How it's assessed

- Ask to see the practice document.
- Confirm it describes what the current practice actually is, not what it was originally intended to be.
- Confirm changes to the practice are reflected in the document.
