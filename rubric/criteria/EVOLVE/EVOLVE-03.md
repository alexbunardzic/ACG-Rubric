# EVOLVE-03 — The practice survives the departure of any single person

## Criterion

If any single person on the team leaves, the practice continues without material degradation. No individual is the sole point of knowledge for how AI-assisted code is produced, reviewed, or verified.

## Why it matters

A practice that depends on one person is not a practice; it is that person's habits. The purpose of a documented, assessed practice is that it outlives its authors.

## How it's assessed

- Identify each role in the practice (specification author, agent operator, reviewer, verifier).
- For each, confirm at least two people can perform it.
- Confirm the practice document is sufficient for a new hire to take on a role without shadowing an incumbent.
