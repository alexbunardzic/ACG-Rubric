## Note on scope difference

NIST AI RMF is a risk-management framework at the organizational level. It covers AI governance, mapping, measurement, and management. The ACG Rubric is narrower: it addresses only the code-generation practice, but does so in more prescriptive detail. Alignment is therefore mostly at the level of NIST's *Govern* and *Manage* functions, with parts of *Measure*.
